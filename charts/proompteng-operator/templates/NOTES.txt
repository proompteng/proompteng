Thank you for installing the Proompteng operator!

To verify the deployment:
  kubectl get pods -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}

Operator configuration is stored in the ConfigMap:
  kubectl get configmap {{ include "proompteng-operator.fullname" . }}-config -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -o yaml

Metrics are exposed on Service {{ include "proompteng-operator.fullname" . }}:{{ .Values.metrics.service.port }}.
